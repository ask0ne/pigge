Get Started with Pigge

Pigge is a digital piggy bank for kids where they can save money in their e-wallets and spend it independently on available services or transfer it to their friends.

About us

Pigge is a digital e-wallet platform for kids of age group 8+ years. It is a freedom enjoyed by kids and trusted platform for parents. At pigge kids learn most important 3's - saving, spending and sharing through our interactive and interesting tips, tricks and quizzes related to money management.

Why do Kids' need Pigge ?

As kids enter into the era of technological advancements with many activites getting digital, they grow up with a pressure to cope with these activities. One such bigger advancement has been seen in the financial industry such as payments through e-wallet. Pigge gives the kids an amazing benefit of having their personal e-wallets for saving their pocket money and spending it for their necessities independently. Parents play a primary role in teaching their kids importance of financial responsibility.To make it easy for them Pigge gives them ability to monitor the kids' wallet activities and approve their transactions.

Features

Read more to know Us better

Kid registration

To register the kid at Pigge for availing wallet and its services only a parent/Guardian can fill in the details of the kid such as their name, date of birth, email-id, etc and provide authentic proof of his/her identity such as ID card for verification.

Personal wallets to save and spend

Kids get access to their personal e-wallets for saving their pocket money and spend it on available services such as food, clothes or any such available services or sharing with their friends.

Parental Control

Pigge provides parents the solo authority to add funds into their kids' account. Parents get to keep an eye on their kids' wallet activities and approve any transaction their kid wishes to make.

Learn, Follow and Master

Pigge aims to teach kids the importance of having financial responsibility and money management for which the kids get access to various interesting tips, tricks and quizzes
